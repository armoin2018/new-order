    Class SanctionsEngine

    Stateless sanctions engine that evaluates SWIFT disconnections, tiered sanctions effects, and sanctions-fatigue progression.

    Every method is a pure function — the engine holds only a reference to the immutable financial configuration section.

    applySanctionTier

    • applySanctionTier(input: SanctionTierInput): SanctionTierResult

      Apply a specific sanction tier and compute economic effects on the target and blowback costs on the imposer.

      All target effects are multiplied by the effectiveness multiplier, which equals 1 − fatigueDecay (clamped to [0, 1]). As sanctions fatigue grows, their impact diminishes.

      Targeted: oligarch power-base hit on target; DI cost on imposer. Sectoral: GDP decay proportional to number of sectors; own-trade reduction on imposer. Comprehensive: GDP decay + treasury hit + civil unrest on target; legitimacy cost on imposer if the target is considered weak.

      Parameters

        Sanction tier application parameters.

    computeSanctionsFatigue

    • computeSanctionsFatigue(input: SanctionsFatigueInput): SanctionsFatigueResult

      Compute the progression of sanctions fatigue for a given turn.

      Fatigue grows each turn by the base decay rate plus an additional factor for each evasion network the target has established.

      newFatigue = clamp(currentFatigue + baseDecay + evasionNetworks × 0.1, 0, 1)

      As fatigue approaches 1, the effectiveness of sanctions approaches 0.

      Parameters

      • input: SanctionsFatigueInput

        Sanctions fatigue computation parameters.

      The updated fatigue state with growth delta and remaining effectiveness.

    evaluateSwiftDisconnection

    • evaluateSwiftDisconnection(
          input: SwiftDisconnectionInput,
      ): SwiftDisconnectionResult

      Evaluate whether a faction is eligible to disconnect a target from the SWIFT international payment network, and compute the resulting economic effects on the target.

      Eligibility: the imposer must meet at least one of:

      • Diplomatic Influence ≥ diThreshold (default 60), or
      • Coalition GDP share ≥ coalitionGDPThreshold (default 0.6).

      Effects when eligible:

      • Target trade income reduced by tradeIncomeReduction (−70 %).
      • Target GDP decays by gdpDecayPerTurn (−5 % / turn).
      • Alternative payment effectiveness grows from 30 % base by +5 % per turn of disconnection, capped at 100 %.

      Parameters

      • input: SwiftDisconnectionInput

        SWIFT disconnection evaluation parameters.

      Returns SwiftDisconnectionResult

      The evaluation result with eligibility, effects, and reason.
